The vacation season is here and this is particularly true for non-resident Indians (NRIs), who visit their homes and families. Vacations are an exorbitant affair and need thorough planning. Sometimes this can be a bitter experience for guests when al they wanted was to relax and spend some quality time with their loved ones.

NRIs with relatives in India could save on accommodation costs but it certainly doesn't offer the same amount of privacy that he/she could enjoy when living by themselves. Therefore, it isn't uncommon to see NRIs choosing a hotel for their accommodation during their stay. Another reason for choosing a hotel is that it allows them to truly experience and explore the essence of India. There are very few hotels that offer both, and Pride Plaza Hotel Aerocity- Delhi is one such hotel!
Delhi is the national capital and it has witnessed important historic and political events in the past few decades. This, paired with the fact that there are plenty of tourist spots, make Delhi a popular vacation destination among NRIs. Another reason why Pride Plaza Hotel Aerocity is a great choice is its location. Situated close to the Delhi international Airport, our hotel aims to provide guests with a hassle-free experience during their stay. Our hotel also offers cab services which will help guests save more time and energy.
Our staff and concierge are extremely polite and guests will surely experience the best that Indian hospitality has to offer. Guests are welcomed with a Tika, a flower garland, and an Aarti. The lobby of the hotel features a ton of modern Indian paintings which exude luxury. Guests will fall in love with the intoxicating scent of tuberose while they listen to the soothing tunes of Indian classical music. The Pride desk will be at their service offering a wide range of services that will ensure a comfortable stay for our NRI guests. Our hotel also offers an assortment of delectable Indian dishes in the form of homemade pickles and jams, chaat items, welcome drink and Tamarind candies.

Pride Plaza Hotel Aerocity will leave a lasting impression on our happy guests as they experience the best of Indian hospitality!
