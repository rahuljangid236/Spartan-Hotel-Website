"Weddings", is the word that gives a scream of glory in many ways for the couples who got engaged and want to spend their life together - they want to create their own fairytale stories which they want to turn into reality through these joyous events. Weddings are a testament to love and commitment, the wedding must be as enchanting as one person's romantic life with their significant other. The wedding rituals and functions are eventually set in place when there is a venue. The wedding venue is one of the most important things, it sets the stage for every other aspect, from decor to multiple cuisines which will reflect the joy and commitment on a special day. It serves as a memory that will be etched onto the hearts for the rest of each other's life and deserves to be nothing short of as enchanting as love and devotion are.
Nowadays people are becoming more opinionated about destination weddings because when it comes to deciding on the wedding venue couple, they prefer to go with a destination one, and especially in India there are many choices from an Opulent palace, a relaxed nuptial by the beach or an intimate wedding tucked away in nature.” These destination weddings have something for everyone. Today's wedding couples are looking and shortlisting for an Instagram - worthy wedding venue that might be one of the biggest pieces of the planning puzzle and Pride Hotels is having these planning puzzle pieces sorted for you” says Atul Upadhyay, Senior Vice President, Pride Hotels Group.
Wedding at Pride” gives you the whole new experience of having a big fat dream wedding and a venue like Pride Plaza Hotel - Ahmedabad, where people can host grand weddings for up to 800 guests. The hotel is equipped with all the modern amenities, along with excellent hospitality and service. It excludes vibrant energy that attracts a bright and ambitious vibe around the world. When it comes to wedding celebrations, the Pride Plaza Hotel- Ahmedabad creates the perfect blend of dreams and luxury that turns into a timeless event to be memorable for all attendees of the wedding. The staff of the hotel put all the effort into making your special day more happening with captivating decor and preparing multi cuisines around the world. So, if you're looking to tie the knot amidst the abundance that the royal city has to offer, Pride Plaza Hotel - Ahmedabad is the perfect bet. Experience royal luxury with a multitude of signature dining experiences, extensive leisure facilities, and a host of rejuvenating recreational facilities.
Indian weddings are traditionally multi-day affairs, and involve many intricate ceremonies, such as the painting of the hands and feet of the bride called a mehndi. Garlands are presented to guests of honor instead of corsages, and lots of flower or rose petals are thrown for good luck.
