TRULY INDIAN TRADITIONALLY LUXURIOUS. PURELY SPARTAN


Spartan Hotels Group is an eminent Indian hospitality brand with a presence in major cities across the country. Envisioned by Mr S. P. SPARTAN HOSPITALITY

Spartan Hotels Group resonate true Indian Hospitality and its warmth can be felt right from the moment a guest steps in.

At Spartan, we start with a traditional welcome of Namaskar and welcome our guests with a Tika, Aarti, and Garland. Spartan Hotel incorporates the Five Sense of traditional Indian welcome namely, fragrance, music, ambience, regional Indian paintings and frescoes, and mouth-watering Indian cuisines. We ensure that all the requirements of our guests are addressed on time by our professional staff, that is always on toes to provide efficient services.

SPARTAN BUSINESS HOTELS

Spartan Hotels Group offer a range of personalized services that keep the modern business traveller content and relaxed. A perfect blend of comfort and convenience await the guests in our aesthetically pleasing rooms, thereby ensuring that our guests experience the ultimate relaxation and comfort. Our business hotels are located in prime locations and are in close proximity to various travel hubs and tourist destinations.
